Egg Paratha

Below is the recipe to prepare "Egg Paratha" a popular Indian household dish. I like this dish because it's pretty simple to make and taste is awesome. I prefer eating it with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • egg
  • onion
  • chilly
  • salt
  • soda
  • water
  • rice oil

Process of cooking

  1. Take flour in beaker 🍔
  2. Add finely sliced onions and chilly 😘
  3. Add salt and very little amount of soda 😷
  4. Add a good amount of water to give it thick slurry-like texture 😃
  5. Mix it well 😎
  6. Polish the surface of the frying pan with little amount of rice oil 😍
  7. Take some of the mixture in a ladle and spread it on the hot pan
  8. Fry it on low heat
  9. Flip it over using a spatula
  10. Do the flip-flops for 4-6 times and then take it out of the pan
